# Sunstone Emotion Substance Variants

Use: source material for implemented emotion-as-substance patterns.

Purpose: provide broad source material for the pattern class where abstract emotion or state is treated as a physical substance, physical motion, or physical weather.

Do not treat this file as golden fixture output.

State: source-only supporting material. Current rule coverage is recorded in `legacy/source-material/incorporation-record.md`.

## Pattern Boundary

Positive signal:

- An abstract emotion or state behaves like liquid, vapor, weather, pressure, weight, heat, or a carried substance.
- The line does not name a concrete source, physical mechanism, or measurable condition.
- The line converts interior emotion or group atmosphere into physical motion.

Reject signal:

- The substance is literal.
- The body fact is medical, measurable, injured, cold, tired, or physically caused.
- The weather is literal.
- The state is caused, measured, diagnosed, or explained by a concrete event.
- The action uses a normal cause clause.

## Implementation Cautions

- This class overlaps with `empty-emotional-weather`, `body-emotion-shorthand`, `abstract-metaphor-claim`, and `silence-as-actor`.
- Do not match literal substances, weather, medical symptoms, measured pressure, measured tension, or caused silence.
- A safe matcher should require an abstract emotion or state slot plus a physical motion, substance, weather, carrier, or container verb.
- Cause clauses and concrete physical sources should be strong reject signals.
