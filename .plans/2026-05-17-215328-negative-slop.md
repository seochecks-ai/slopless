# Negative Slop

## Goal

Expand `syntactic-patterns:negation-reframe` to catch negative framing used as a reveal device in prose, without creating a new family or a duplicate rule.

## Approach

1. Extend the existing negation matcher under `src/rules/syntactic-patterns/contrast/private/`.
2. Add constrained support for:
   - `X was no longer Y. X/It was Z.`
   - `Not a/an X. It was Y.`
   - short `did not X. Instead, Y.` or `did not X. Same-subject Y.`
3. Keep because/until factual negations as no-hits.
4. Add line cases under `cases/syntactic-patterns`.
5. Mirror the new cases into existing corpus prose if missing.
6. Accept fixture output only after reviewing new findings.
